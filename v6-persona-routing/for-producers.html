Common Event Types

How producers use Norsk Studio

Common event scenarios and how Norsk Studio handles them — with room for your production story as we grow together.

Sports Producer

Multi-camera sports broadcast with real-time source switching

A live sports producer needed to manage six camera inputs, switch between sources in real time, apply picture-in-picture for replays, and deliver simultaneously to a CDN and a venue's internal distribution — all from a single operator interface.

A single Norsk Studio workflow handled all six inputs, source switching, PiP composition, and dual-destination output. The producer controlled everything from one visual interface during the live event, with per-input monitoring visible throughout.

Talk to us about your next event →
News Operations

24/7 news channel with AI-powered stream monitoring

A news operations team running a 24/7 channel couldn't staff continuous manual monitoring overnight. They needed the system to detect signal issues, trigger alerts, and switch to backup sources automatically — without human intervention.

Norsk Reasoning monitored the stream continuously, detected anomalies in real time, and triggered automated failover and alerts without operator intervention overnight. The operations team reviewed decisions each morning and refined detection rules over time.

Talk to us about your channel →
Live Events Team

Corporate event with distributed remote contributors

A corporate live events team needed to bring in six remote presenters via WebRTC alongside on-site cameras, mix them into a single output stream, and deliver to the company's internal platform and a public streaming destination simultaneously.

Remote presenters joined via browser-based WebRTC — no specialist hardware on their end. Norsk mixed remote and on-site inputs in the workflow with real-time monitoring for each contributor. Both destinations received the same output from a single encoding pass.
