Deployment Scenarios

How broadcasters deploy Norsk Studio

Common patterns from organisations modernising live broadcast infrastructure — with room for your case study as we grow.

Multi-Channel Broadcaster

OTT launch without touching the broadcast chain

A regional broadcaster needed to add an OTT streaming tier alongside existing playout infrastructure — without any risk to the broadcast chain that clients depended on for zero-failure delivery.

Norsk deployed as a hybrid layer alongside the existing chain. Broadcast playout ran untouched; Norsk handled OTT ingest, encoding, and packaging independently. OTT launched with no broadcast incidents.

Sports Rights Holder

Multi-CDN delivery from a single production workflow

A sports media company needed to deliver live events to multiple CDN partners simultaneously — each with different format and latency requirements — without duplicating infrastructure for each distribution endpoint.

One Norsk workflow handled simultaneous HLS, DASH, and WebRTC packaging with per-destination output configuration. A single ingest and encode path fed all distribution endpoints, eliminating redundant infrastructure per partner.

Enterprise Broadcaster

Channel consolidation with GPU-accelerated processing

An enterprise media organisation was running dedicated encoding hardware per channel. Scaling to new channels meant hardware procurement cycles that couldn't keep pace with business demands.

Norsk Spectrum's GPU-accelerated standards conversion replaced multiple single-purpose appliances. Higher channel density on shared infrastructure, faster deployment of new channels, at lower cost per channel.

Business Case

The ROI of modernising with Norsk Studio

The cost of broadcast disruption far exceeds the cost of prevention. Norsk Studio's hybrid approach lets you modernise without betting your broadcast on it.

  • →
    Reduce tooling sprawl and cost per channel

    Replace multiple single-purpose tools with one platform that handles ingest, processing, monitoring, and delivery. Norsk Spectrum's GPU-accelerated standards conversion delivers higher channel density at significantly lower cost per channel than dedicated hardware or CPU-based approaches.

  • →
    Faster feature delivery

    New broadcast capabilities that used to take quarters now take days. No vendor roadmap dependency.

  • →
    Protect existing infrastructure investment

    Hybrid deployment means your existing broadcast hardware doesn't become stranded assets overnight.

  • →
    Operational risk reduction — including AI automation

    Sub-second failover, real-time monitoring, and fault-tolerant architecture reduce the operational risk your teams carry every broadcast. Norsk Reasoning adds an AI layer that monitors streams and acts autonomously — switching cameras, triggering recordings, alerting on-call — eliminating entire categories of manual monitoring work.
